# Game Concept

## Title

Gyaru Rhythm Battle Game

## Pitch

A C++/SFML rhythm-first dating simulator inspired by 2000s Flash and DS fashion games. The player enters a Japanese school/dorm social scene as a confident gyaru character and resolves one rivalry through dialogue choices and a rhythm battle.

## Vertical Slice

The first version is one complete, playable scene:

- one rival, Yumi
- one pre-battle dialogue choice
- one four-lane rhythm chart
- one post-battle result
- relationship, reputation, and drama-risk variables

The player can improve or damage the social outcome through both dialogue and rhythm performance.

## Tone

The tone is stylized social satire. Drama is represented through abstract variables and dialogue outcomes, not through complex or explicit simulation.

## Deferred Ideas

These are intentionally postponed:

- school map
- dorm management
- wardrobe/dress-up system
- multiple romance routes
- procedural gossip
- AI dialogue
- chart editor
- extra songs
- fighting-game mechanics
- life/generation simulation

The project should not spend serious time on these until the one-battle loop is reliable.
